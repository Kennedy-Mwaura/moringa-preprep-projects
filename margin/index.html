Udacity

Acording to Wikipedis, Udacity is "Udacity a for-profit educational organisation founded by Sebastian Thrun, David Stavens, and Mike Sokolsky offering masssive open online courses (MOOCS) "

put simply, udacity is an online learning platform offering free or cheap access to quality education in computer science. It is a resource that I encourage anyone interested in software technology to check out. If you SPOC, Udacity is for you.
