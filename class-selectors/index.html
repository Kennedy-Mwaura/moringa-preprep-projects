Featuring Kijabe

Pine trees belong to a family of trees called conifer.

Geographical Distribution

Most regions of the Northern hemisphere host some native species of pines. More info. This doesn't mean that pine trees do not grow in the southern hemisphere.
Take Kijabe for example.

Why Kijabe

Kijabe is in the Sounthern Hemisphere, but has a diverse population of pine trees.

Is this weird?

Not exactly.

Weird explanation: Geographically speaking, the earth rotates on a tilted axis, which means that for almost half of the day, Kijabe is in the Northern Hemisphere (at least if we consider the cosmic plane).

Really, though, we started by saying that most pine trees grow on the Northern Hemisphere. But does not mean that no pine trees grow in the southern hemisphere.
